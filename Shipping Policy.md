**Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)**  
  
**1. Are your products handmade?**  
Yes, all our resin creations are handmade with love and care.  
**2. Do you accept custom orders?**  
Yes! We love creating personalized pieces. Contact us for custom requests.  
**3. How long do custom orders take?**  
Typically 7–14 business days depending on customization.  
**4. Can I cancel an order?**  
Orders can not be cancelled.   
**5. Do you ship all over India?**  
Yes, we offer pan-India shipping.** **  
**6.Do you ship across India / internationally?**  
We currently ship across India. International shipping availability can be confirmed upon request.  
**7. What should I do if my order arrives damaged?**  
Please contact us within 48 hours of delivery with photos or an unboxing video for assistance.  
**8. Are slight variations or imperfections normal in handmade products?**  
Yes. Minor variations in color, texture, or finish are part of the handmade process and make each piece unique.  
**9.Do you accept returns or exchanges?**  
As most products are handmade or customized, returns and exchanges are generally not accepted unless the item arrives damaged or incorrect.  
**10. How can I contact you for support or custom inquiries?**  
You can reach us through our Contact page, email, or Instagram for assistance.  
  
  
**Returns & Refund Policy**  
  
Due to the handmade and customized nature of our products, we do not accept returns or exchanges.  
  
However, if you receive a damaged or incorrect item, please contact us within **48 hours of delivery** with photos/video proof.  
After review, we may offer:  
* Replacement  
* Refund (if applicable)  
Customized orders are non-refundable unless damaged during transit.

Our Story

Behind the Brand

Founded by Srishti Bhimrajka in Kolkata, Resinn Craftss is the culmination of an artistic soul's journey. What began as a passion for poetic expression and design has transformed into a purpose-driven venture that resonates with creativity and elegance.

Srishti is a self-made resin artist and entrepreneur who believes in creating meaningful, lasting art. Every piece — from elegant home décor to functional nameplates — is handcrafted with utmost care and a vision to bring value to your home.

Beyond the workshop, Srishti is a passionate mentor, conducting affordable resin art classes to empower aspiring artists. She shares her journey, from mastering precision in resin to building a loyal online presence, to help others turn their passion into a profession.
